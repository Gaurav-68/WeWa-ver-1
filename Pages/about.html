About WeWa - WeatherWatch

This project is developed by Gaurav C. Pidurkar & Ritika Kale during our first year of Post-Graduation in M.C.A.


With the rise in climate change and global warming, there is a growing need to stay informed about sudden weather shifts. Unfortunately, staying updated can often be overwhelming.


WeWa - WeatherWatch aims to bridge the gap between individuals and critical weather updates by providing a simple, intuitive, and informative platform.
